My Roots in Chongqing

I’m Dawei or David Luo and I was born on October 28, 2008, in the vibrant city of Chongqing, China. A year before I made my entrance into the world, my parents had already adopted our family dog, so I've literally never known life without a furry companion by my side. For the first five years, it was just me and my parents, until my brother was born and suddenly I had a new role to figure out as the older sibling. Growing up in such a massive, energetic city definitely shaped me, filling my early memories with unique sights and sounds. My Life as a Junior at CCA

Right now, I'm a junior at Canyon Crest Academy, trying to balance a pretty packed schedule with everything else I love to do. My classes this year include US History, Intro to Calculus, AP Computer Science Principles, and Chinese 4, which keeps me pretty busy between homework and projects. In my free time, I love playing badminton—it's the perfect way to clear my head and have some fun competing. I'm also a huge car enthusiast; I could spend hours watching videos about different models or just appreciating the design of cars I see around town. When I'm at home, I'm usually hanging out with my four cats, who I absolutely adore and who definitely run the household. What I'm Driving Toward

When I think about my future, my dream is to attend the University of Washington and major in Business. I'm really drawn to the idea of understanding how companies work and maybe even starting something of my own someday. But I'm also a practical person, so I have a backup plan I genuinely care about: if business doesn't work out, I want to become a police officer and serve my community. No matter what career I end up in though, one goal is non-negotiable—I will own a Mazda Miata someday. To me, it's not just a car; it's the perfect combination of style and pure driving joy.
